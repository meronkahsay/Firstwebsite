First Week as a Student at AkraChix:A Mix of Excitement and Challenges

You're wondering what AkiraChix is and what i will talk about, right? No worries i will tell about it here.

Akirachix is a one-year fully sponsored residential hands-on technical training program.
it is the place where i'll be staying for the next 10 months. if you want to know more, you can visit their website: AkiraChix.com

Its been a week since i joined the organization. The first week at AkiraChix was a complicated emotion.I felt overwhelmed.
Initialy it was exciting to try new things and capture the moment by taking picture of my self and others.
However adapting to new enviroment,meeting a diverse group of people,dealing with homesickness was somehow challenging.

The Good Things I Enjoyed

  1. Its neat,the place it self makes you feel refreshed.
  2. it only allows you to focus on your studies.
  3. it push you hard to become disciplined and organized.
  4. it does not allow you to be left behind.

The things I was Dissapointed with

  • the cleaning part on weekend: ahh don't get me started with this..even though its best for us, i think it would be better if they sceduled it all on saturday or Sunday.
    its becoming hard to do my assignment or give myself some time.
  • waking up at 5:00 am really,cause i did not get used to that.
  • the are no private study rooms available when you need privacy for yourself.

Despite all the good and bad things that have happened, I am enjoying my time at AkiraChix. I am improving my self over time and trying to make new friends.
It can be hard sometimes but this is the sacrifie i am making for better tommorow. and finally i miss injera!
